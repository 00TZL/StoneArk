The Australian Dollar (AUD) continued its downward trend on Friday, marking a third consecutive day of losses against the US Dollar (USD). Weighed down by widespread risk aversion, the currency struggled to find support as global equities and other risk-sensitive assets saw significant selling pressure.  

The broader market sentiment remained cautious, with investors retreating to safer assets amid mounting concerns over global economic conditions. This risk-off environment has kept the AUD under pressure, highlighting its vulnerability to shifts in market sentiment and global risk appetite. Traders are closely monitoring upcoming economic data and central bank signals for further clues on the outlook for the AUD.
