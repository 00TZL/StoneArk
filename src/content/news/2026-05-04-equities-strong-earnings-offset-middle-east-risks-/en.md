Equities closed last week on a positive note, driven by strong earnings in the tech and growth sectors, according to the Danske Research Team. Despite concerns around rising Iran-related risks and increased oil prices, which dampened sentiment, the markets were able to maintain their upward momentum. This strong performance highlights the resilience of corporate earnings amid external pressures.

The team also noted a return of the negative correlation between equities and bonds, with yields climbing in tandem with stock prices. This shift signals a change in market dynamics, as investors respond to economic data and geopolitical tensions, creating an interesting contrast with previous market trends.
