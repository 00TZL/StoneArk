The USD/CHF pair is weakening, currently hovering near 0.7805 in the early European session on Wednesday. The Swiss Franc is gaining strength against the US Dollar as investors turn to safe-haven assets amid rising tensions in the Middle East. 

The escalating geopolitical concerns have led to increased demand for the Swiss Franc, traditionally viewed as a safe-haven currency. As a result, the USD/CHF pair has lost momentum, reflecting broader risk-off sentiment in global markets. Traders are closely monitoring the situation, with potential volatility ahead depending on how the situation in the Middle East evolves.
