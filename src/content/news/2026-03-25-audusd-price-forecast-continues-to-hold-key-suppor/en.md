The AUD/USD pair remains under pressure, trading 0.3% lower at approximately 0.6975 during the late Asian session on Wednesday. The Australian dollar continues to face challenges, unable to maintain upward momentum, as it hovers near key support around the 0.6900 mark. This level remains crucial for the pair’s near-term outlook, with traders keenly observing whether it holds in the coming days.

Despite the downward pressure, the pair’s performance has been relatively stable in recent sessions, holding above the 0.6900 support. A break below this level could lead to further declines, with next support zones likely around 0.6850. However, a rebound from the current levels could pave the way for a recovery towards the 0.7000 resistance.
