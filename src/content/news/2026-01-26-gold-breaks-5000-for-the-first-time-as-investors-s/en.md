Gold (XAU/USD) has surged past the $5,000 mark for the first time, marking a new milestone in its ongoing rally. On Monday, the precious metal broke through this psychological threshold as investors flocked to safe-haven assets amid rising geopolitical tensions and growing economic instability.

The rally comes as concerns over global inflation, supply chain disruptions, and escalating geopolitical risks continue to weigh on market sentiment. With economic uncertainty showing no signs of abating, gold has become a preferred refuge for investors seeking protection from volatile markets, driving its price to unprecedented levels.
