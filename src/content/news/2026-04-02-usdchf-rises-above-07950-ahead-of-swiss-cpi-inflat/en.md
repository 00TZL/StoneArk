The USD/CHF pair surged to approximately 0.7985 in early European trading on Thursday, marking a notable increase in the value of the US Dollar against the Swiss Franc (CHF). This rise comes ahead of the release of Switzerland’s Consumer Price Index (CPI) inflation data, which is expected to offer insights into the country’s economic health.

The Greenback's strength against the Swiss Franc follows a speech by US President Donald Trump, which appears to have bolstered market confidence in the US economy. Traders are closely watching the upcoming Swiss CPI data for any potential surprises that could influence the pair’s next moves in the market.
