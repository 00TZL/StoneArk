The EUR/JPY currency pair is struggling below the 183.00 mark, trading around 182.95 during the early European session on Monday. The Japanese Yen (JPY) has gained momentum against the Euro (EUR) as the escalating conflict in the Middle East increases demand for safe-haven assets.

Investors are closely watching the situation in the Middle East, which has lifted the JPY as market sentiment shifts toward risk aversion. Meanwhile, the German January Industrial Production data, set to be released later on Monday, could provide further insight into the Eurozone's economic outlook and potentially influence EUR/JPY movements in the coming hours.
