The Canadian Dollar (CAD) remained under pressure on Wednesday, trading near its recent lows as the US Dollar (USD) held steady. Investors continue to favor the Greenback amid elevated US yields, keeping the CAD on the defensive. The USD/CAD pair hovered around 1.3724, just shy of a four-week peak, reflecting cautious sentiment in the Canadian currency ahead of key economic updates.

Market watchers note that the limited movement in the pair suggests traders are weighing the impact of higher US returns against domestic factors in Canada. While the Canadian Dollar faces downward pressure, any shifts in risk appetite or central bank guidance could prompt renewed volatility, making the near-term outlook closely tied to US interest rate dynamics and broader market trends.
