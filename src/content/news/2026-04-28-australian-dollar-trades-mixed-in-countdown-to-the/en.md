The Australian Dollar (AUD) showed a mixed performance ahead of key economic events this week, with the currency trading slightly lower against the US Dollar (USD). During the European trading session on Tuesday, the AUD was seen at 0.7180, marking a modest dip in value.

Market participants are awaiting the release of the Australian Consumer Price Index (CPI), which could offer fresh insights into inflationary pressures. Additionally, the upcoming Federal Reserve policy decision is adding further uncertainty, with traders positioning themselves ahead of potential moves that could influence the AUD’s trajectory in the near term.
