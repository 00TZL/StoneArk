On Wednesday, June 24, the US Dollar extended its rally, climbing to a fresh 13-month high as buyers continued to dominate global FX markets. The move reflected persistent demand for the greenback amid shifting rate expectations and broad risk aversion across major currencies. Traders noted steady momentum throughout the Asian and European sessions, reinforcing bullish sentiment.

Analysts said the advance was supported by higher US Treasury yields and expectations that interest rates will remain elevated for longer. Major peers remained under pressure, with the euro and yen both weakening against the dollar. Market participants will now watch upcoming economic data for signals on whether the rally can extend further into the week. Volatility is expected to stay elevated this week.
