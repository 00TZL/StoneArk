Gold (XAU/USD) has shown a strong rebound from the mid-$4,600s region, recovering from a four-day low hit during the Asian session on Friday. The precious metal found support around $4,655, aided by a weaker risk sentiment in global markets, which pushed investors toward safer assets. 

Despite the bounce, gold struggles to maintain upward momentum, as it faces resistance from broader market conditions. The shift in risk tone, coupled with growing expectations of a potential Federal Reserve rate cut, has created an uncertain environment for gold, leaving its next moves highly dependent on broader economic data and market sentiment.
