U.S. equity futures edged lower on Friday, reflecting a cautious sentiment ahead of the release of the highly anticipated Nonfarm Payrolls report. Dow Jones futures slipped 0.04%, hovering around 51,650, while S&P 500 futures dropped 0.64% to approximately 7,550. The decline highlights investor hesitancy as markets brace for fresh labor data that could influence Federal Reserve policy expectations and broader economic forecasts.  

Tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 futures took a more pronounced hit, plunging 1.23% to near 30,110 during European trading hours. Market participants remain watchful ahead of the regular U.S. session, with volatility expected to rise once payroll figures are published. Traders are weighing recent economic signals against potential shifts in monetary policy, prompting a cautious approach to risk assets.
