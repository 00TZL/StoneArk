Dow Jones futures climbed 0.24% to trade above 53,300 in early Friday European hours, as softer economic data eased expectations for aggressive Federal Reserve tightening. S&P 500 futures also advanced 0.46%, moving near 7,560, while Nasdaq 100 futures outperformed with a 1.0% gain, trading around 29,850.

Currency markets reflected improved risk appetite, with traders pricing in reduced odds of further Fed rate hikes after the softer macro releases. The US dollar eased slightly against major peers, while Treasury yields stabilized, supporting equity-linked sentiment. Investors remain focused on upcoming labor and inflation data, which will guide expectations for the policy path. Analysts note that positioning is increasingly sensitive to any additional signs of cooling growth momentum across the US economy outlook.
