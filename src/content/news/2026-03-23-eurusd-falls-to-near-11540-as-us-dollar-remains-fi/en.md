The EUR/USD pair is trading lower, down 0.2% to around 1.1545 during the late Asian session on Monday. The euro remains under pressure as the US Dollar (USD) strengthens in response to rising demand for safe-haven assets. 

Escalating conflicts in the Middle East have prompted market uncertainty, driving investors towards the dollar as a safer option. The ongoing geopolitical tensions have added to the market's cautious sentiment, leaving the euro vulnerable to further declines as the USD maintains its firm position. Traders will be closely monitoring developments in the region for any impact on broader market sentiment.
