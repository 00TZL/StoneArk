Gold prices remain steady above the $4,500 level during Thursday’s Asian session, with the metal struggling to gain momentum as markets digest firm expectations of a hawkish Federal Reserve stance. The lack of fresh bullish catalysts, combined with elevated US yields, continues to cap upside potential, keeping XAU/USD confined within a narrow range.

The precious metal’s price action reflects ongoing consolidation, holding above the key psychological threshold while failing to recover from the recent rejection near the 100-day Simple Moving Average (SMA). This stalled rebound suggests cautious sentiment among traders, as stronger monetary tightening expectations act as a persistent headwind for non-yielding assets like gold.
