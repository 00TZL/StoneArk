GBP/JPY struggles to extend the previous day's solid intraday bounce from the vicinity of a one-month trough touched last week, edging lower on Tuesday. Spot prices lack bearish conviction, however, and trade just below the 214.00 mark amid mixed fundamental cues.

UK politics and renewed intervention fears continue to weigh on sentiment, keeping traders cautious about further upside in the cross. Market participants now turn their focus to upcoming UK PMIs for fresh directional cues, which could help clarify growth momentum and expectations for Bank of England policy outlook in the near term. Investors will also monitor any official commentary for signals on currency volatility management, with intervention speculation likely to keep volatility elevated through the week ahead of data.
