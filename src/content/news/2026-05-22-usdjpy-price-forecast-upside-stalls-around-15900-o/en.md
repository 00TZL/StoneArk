The USD/JPY pair edged slightly higher to 159.10 during Friday’s European session, as traders remain cautious amid expectations of a potential US-Iran agreement. Over the past three trading days, the pair has largely moved sideways, reflecting a market awaiting confirmation on geopolitical developments. Investors appear reluctant to commit to strong positions until more clarity emerges regarding the diplomatic outcome.

Market sentiment is being shaped by hopes that a deal could ease tensions and influence risk appetite. While the upside is limited around the 159.00 level, traders are closely monitoring updates from both Washington and Tehran. Any concrete announcement could prompt renewed volatility in the USD/JPY, with traders weighing the implications for broader currency market dynamics.
