Silver prices (XAG/USD) are struggling to make headway, holding near $75.50 after dropping to a fresh monthly low during the late Asian session on Thursday. Despite firm expectations that the Federal Reserve will extend its pause on interest rate hikes, silver remains under pressure, failing to capitalize on the dovish market sentiment.

The $75.50 level marks a key support for silver, with traders eyeing potential volatility in the coming sessions. The ongoing uncertainty surrounding global economic conditions and central bank policy decisions continues to weigh on precious metals. However, any further signs of Fed hesitation could provide the catalyst for a rebound if silver can break through resistance above current levels.
