The US Dollar showed renewed strength on Friday, with the US Dollar Index (DXY) trading near 99.40 during Asian session hours. The index, which measures the greenback against a basket of six major currencies, gained momentum following the US Federal Reserve’s decision to hold rates steady in a hawkish stance. Investors interpreted the Fed’s signal as a commitment to maintaining tight monetary policy, bolstering demand for the dollar across global markets.  

Heightened geopolitical tensions in the Middle East also added support to the USD as traders sought safe-haven assets. Market participants are now closely watching the DXY for potential moves toward the 99.50 mark, reflecting both Fed guidance and ongoing risk-off sentiment. Volatility may persist as developments unfold.
