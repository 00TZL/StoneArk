The USD/JPY pair retraces its early gains, edging slightly lower to around 159.20 during the late Asian session on Monday. The move comes as the US Dollar (USD) loses momentum, despite ongoing tensions in US-Iran diplomacy, which remain at a standstill. The currency pair had initially risen, but the reversal signals caution ahead of key events this week.

As the Bank of Japan (BoJ) and Federal Reserve (Fed) policy meetings approach, the USD/JPY is testing a critical breakout region around 159.2. Traders are closely monitoring any shifts in the monetary outlook from both central banks, with potential volatility expected as these decisions unfold later in the week.
