The Australian Dollar edged higher against the US Dollar on Friday, with the AUD/USD pair hovering near the 0.6900 mark during early European trading. Market participants attribute the uptick to the Reserve Bank of Australia’s hawkish stance, which continues to support the AUD amid broader currency fluctuations. Traders are weighing the impact of the upcoming US jobs report, expected to inject volatility into the markets later in the session.  

Liquidity appears subdued, as trading volumes remain light due to the Good Friday holiday across key markets. Investors are likely to adopt a cautious approach, keeping a close eye on both domestic monetary signals from the RBA and the US employment data, which could steer the AUD/USD trajectory in the near term.
