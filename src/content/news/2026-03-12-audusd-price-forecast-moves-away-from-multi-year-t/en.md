The AUD/USD pair faces selling pressure during the Asian session on Thursday, pulling back from a multi-year high reached the day before. After hitting the 0.7185 mark, the pair seems to have ended its four-day rally, which had brought it to its highest level since June 2022.

As the US dollar strengthens, the Australian dollar starts to lose ground, with the pair sliding toward the 0.7125 level. This shift suggests a temporary setback for AUD/USD, with market participants awaiting further direction amid broader USD strength. The pair's performance in the coming sessions will depend on developments in the US dollar and risk sentiment.
