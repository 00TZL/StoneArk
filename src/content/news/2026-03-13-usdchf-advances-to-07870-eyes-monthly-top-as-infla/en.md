The USD/CHF pair extends its rally for the fourth consecutive day, reaching the 0.7870 level during the Asian session on Friday. This move brings the pair closer to its monthly high, as the US dollar gains momentum ahead of the release of the US PCE data.

Inflation concerns continue to support the USD, fueling expectations of a potential shift in the Federal Reserve's stance. As market participants prepare for the PCE data, which is a key gauge of inflation, the greenback strengthens, providing further support to the USD/CHF pair's bullish trend. Traders are eyeing the monthly high as the next key target for the pair.
