Silver prices (XAG/USD) are holding steady around $74.00 during the late Asian session on Thursday. The metal’s price action remains subdued as market participants await developments in the ongoing geopolitical tensions. The upcoming round of talks between the United States and Iran, set to take place in Pakistan on Saturday, is keeping traders on edge.

The uncertainty surrounding the discussions, focused on a potential permanent ceasefire, is limiting significant price movement for silver. As global markets watch closely, the outcome of these talks could influence risk sentiment and, subsequently, silver's price direction in the near term. Until more clarity emerges, XAG/USD is expected to maintain its flat trading range.
