Gold (XAU/USD) is gaining momentum, continuing the rebound from the previous session’s dip below the $5,000 mark, its lowest level in over a week. The precious metal is drawing support as investors seek safe-haven assets amid rising geopolitical tensions. 

Despite gold’s recent gains, the strength of the U.S. dollar is limiting its upside potential. The greenback’s resilience in global markets is keeping a lid on further price advances, making it difficult for gold to maintain significant upward momentum in the short term. Investors are closely monitoring developments in geopolitical events and USD performance to gauge future price direction.
