The Australian Dollar (AUD) is under pressure, with AUD/JPY falling to near 109.50 following a two-day rally. As geopolitical risks weigh on the currency, concerns over global instability have started to impact the Aussie. 

During the Asian trading session on Thursday, AUD/JPY was seen hovering around 109.60, marking a noticeable decline. The pair’s retreat highlights the ongoing vulnerability of the Australian Dollar as traders remain cautious in light of escalating geopolitical tensions, which are dampening sentiment in the broader market.
