The British Pound is facing upside risks, according to UOB's Quek Ser Leang, despite a recent stall just below the crucial resistance level of 1.3465. The GBP/USD pair closed the trading session near 1.3410, indicating some hesitance in pushing higher.

Intraday momentum indicators have begun to flatten, suggesting that while the potential for upward movement remains, the momentum may be losing steam. Traders will be closely monitoring these developments, as any sustained movement above 1.3465 could signal a stronger bullish trend, while a failure to break this resistance might lead to a consolidation phase for the currency pair.
