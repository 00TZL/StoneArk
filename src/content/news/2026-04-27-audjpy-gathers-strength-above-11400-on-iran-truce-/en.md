The AUD/JPY pair is gaining momentum above the 114.00 mark, reaching around 114.15 during the early European session on Monday. This move follows positive market sentiment fueled by reports that Iran has proposed a truce to the US, aimed at reopening the Strait of Hormuz and bringing an end to the ongoing conflict. 

The news has helped lift broader risk sentiment, benefiting currencies like the Australian Dollar (AUD), which is typically more sensitive to global risk appetite. Investors are closely watching the situation, as a potential de-escalation in the Middle East could further support the AUD and other riskier assets in the near term.
