The USD/CAD pair is showing signs of vulnerability near the 1.3660 level after a modest rise to around 1.3680 during Monday’s Asian session. Traders note that while the pair attempted to extend gains, it has faced selling pressure, limiting upside momentum. Market participants are keeping an eye on intraday support, suggesting cautious positioning ahead of key technical levels.  

The 200-hour EMA remains a critical pivot for bulls, as a sustained move above it could reignite upside potential. Conversely, failure to hold above this moving average may open the door for further downside. Overall, the pair is trading in a tentative range, with short-term sentiment balanced between modest buying interest and cautious selling near resistance.
