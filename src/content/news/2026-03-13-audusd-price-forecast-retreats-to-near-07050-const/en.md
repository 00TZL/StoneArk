The AUD/USD pair experiences a pullback to around 0.7060 during the early European session on Friday. After approaching three-year highs, the pair faces pressure due to growing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. These developments have led to increased demand for safe-haven assets, benefiting the US Dollar (USD).

Despite the recent retreat, the broader outlook for the pair remains constructive. The Australian Dollar is supported by solid domestic data and commodity price trends, suggesting potential for further upside. However, the prevailing market risk sentiment and US Dollar strength could limit the pace of any recovery, keeping the 0.7050-0.7100 range in focus for now.
