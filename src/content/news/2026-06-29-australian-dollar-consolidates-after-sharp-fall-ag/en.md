AUD/USD is consolidating after a sharp fall, with United Overseas Bank strategist Quek Ser Leang noting that the pair remains trapped in a short-term range between 0.6880 and 0.6920 following a recent impulsive decline.

Downward momentum is starting to ease, while analysts suggest a break above 0.6940 would signal that the major 0.6835 support is out of reach, shifting the near-term bias toward stabilization rather than further extension of the recent sell-off. For now the pair is expected to remain range-bound as traders monitor US dollar direction and broader risk sentiment across Asian FX markets, with resistance capped near the upper boundary of the recent consolidation zone. UOB maintains near-term neutral bias within this corridor in the current outlook phase.
