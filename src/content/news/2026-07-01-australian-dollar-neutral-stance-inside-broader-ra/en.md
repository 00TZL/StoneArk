The Australian Dollar (AUD) has shown a notable rebound against the US Dollar (USD), climbing sharply from a low of 0.6867 to a current level of 0.6930. According to UOB’s Quek Ser Leang, this upward movement appears to be overextended, suggesting that traders should exercise caution as the pair navigates within a broader range.

Looking ahead, intraday trading is anticipated to remain confined between 0.6890 and 0.6930, with a key resistance level identified at 0.6935. Market participants are advised to monitor these levels closely, as they may signal potential shifts in momentum for the AUD/USD currency pair in the near term.
