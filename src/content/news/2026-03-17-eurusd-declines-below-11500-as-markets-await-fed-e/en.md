The EUR/USD pair is trading in negative territory, dipping below the 1.1500 mark during the early European session on Tuesday. Currently, the pair hovers around 1.1490 as market participants remain cautious ahead of upcoming central bank decisions. The euro faces pressure from mounting uncertainty regarding interest rate policies in both the U.S. and the Eurozone.

Investors are closely watching the Federal Reserve and European Central Bank's upcoming rate decisions, which are expected to influence the direction of the currency pair. The Fed's stance on tightening monetary policy and the ECB's approach to inflation will likely dictate the near-term movement of EUR/USD. Traders will be awaiting any signals for future policy changes as both banks' decisions loom large on the horizon.
