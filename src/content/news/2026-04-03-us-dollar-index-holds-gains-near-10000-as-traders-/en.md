The US Dollar Index (DXY) is maintaining its gains near the 100.00 mark in early European trading on Friday, supported by a mix of geopolitical and economic factors. Investors are keeping a close eye on US jobs data, with expectations that labor market strength could reinforce the dollar’s appeal. The index, which measures the USD against a basket of six major currencies, has held steady despite mixed signals from other risk assets.  

Heightened tensions in the Middle East, particularly concerns over a prolonged conflict in Iran, have added to safe-haven demand for the greenback. Traders are weighing the potential impact on global markets, keeping the dollar resilient against its peers. Market participants remain cautious ahead of key economic releases later in the week.
