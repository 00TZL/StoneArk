EUR/USD has slightly recovered from its recent declines, hovering near 1.1630 in Asian trading on Wednesday. The pair’s rebound comes after a session of losses, but technical indicators suggest caution. On the daily chart, EUR/USD remains confined within a descending channel, signaling that sellers still have the upper hand. Market watchers note that the nine-day EMA is acting as a near-term resistance, with the 1.1650 level in focus for potential upside tests.  

Traders are monitoring whether the euro can break above this barrier, which could indicate a short-term pause in the downtrend. However, the overall bearish momentum persists, and any rally may remain limited unless the pair decisively clears the channel and EMA resistance.
