The AUD/JPY pair has dropped to 110.00 as a surge in Japanese yen strength overshadows a positive Australian Q4 GDP report. Despite the upbeat data, the cross struggles to maintain upward momentum after yesterday’s brief recovery from the mid-109.00s, the weekly low. 

Market participants have been quick to react to the yen's strengthening, which has pressured the AUD/JPY pair lower. The Australian GDP figures showed resilience in the economy, but this wasn't enough to offset the yen's broader appeal, leading the pair to retreat back to key support levels around 110.00.
