EUR/GBP has stabilized near 0.8650, recovering from earlier losses as it trades around 0.8670 during the Asian session on Monday. The pair saw a gap down at the open, reflecting the Euro's struggle against the British Pound amid heightened global uncertainty.

Traders are adopting a cautious stance, driven by growing concerns over the escalating conflict in the Middle East, which has fueled demand for safe-haven assets. The risk-off sentiment has put pressure on the Euro, while the Pound shows relative strength, keeping EUR/GBP under pressure. Market participants are closely monitoring developments in the region, with volatility likely to persist in the near term.
