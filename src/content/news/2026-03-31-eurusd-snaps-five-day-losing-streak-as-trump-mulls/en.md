The EUR/USD pair has broken its five-day losing streak, gaining momentum as it moves closer to the 1.1475 mark during the Asian trading session on Tuesday. The pair is benefiting from a slight pullback in the US Dollar (USD), which has been under pressure amid growing optimism regarding a potential resolution to the ongoing conflict in the Middle East.

The market sentiment has shifted, with traders hopeful that former U.S. President Donald Trump's consideration to end the war in Iran could lead to a de-escalation. This development has triggered a retreat in the USD, providing support for the Euro and helping EUR/USD recover some ground.
