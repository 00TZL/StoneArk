Silver (XAG/USD) is resuming its downward trajectory on Tuesday after a brief recovery on Monday. The precious metal is under pressure as geopolitical tensions escalate, with Iran dismissing any involvement in de-escalation talks with the United States concerning the ongoing conflict in the Middle East. This denial has weighed heavily on market sentiment, fueling concerns over further instability in the region.

At the time of writing, XAG/USD is trading down 3.2%, hovering just above the $66.90 level. Investors are cautious as global risk sentiment weakens, and silver, often viewed as a safe-haven asset, is reflecting the growing uncertainty in the market.
