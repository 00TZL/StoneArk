The exchange rate is up nearly 0.73% on the day, pushing cable to its strongest level since September 18, 2025, and marking a clear four-month high amid shifting market sentiment.

The Dollar has also struggled against peers such as the EUR, reinforcing the view that capital flows are rotating away from the US currency. This dynamic has allowed the British Pound to outperform, supported by firm demand and favorable trading conditions.
