GBP/USD managed to pause its five-day slide, holding steady around the 1.3200 level in early Asian trading on Tuesday. The pair's recent rebound offers some respite, though momentum remains fragile as traders weigh broader market sentiment and potential economic data releases. Market participants are eyeing key support and resistance levels near 1.3150 and 1.3250, respectively, which could influence short-term price action.  

From a technical perspective, the daily chart continues to show a bearish bias. The pair is moving within a descending channel, suggesting that while short-term gains may persist, the overall trend favors further downside. Traders are likely to monitor for a decisive break above the channel to signal a potential shift in momentum.
