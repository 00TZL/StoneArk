USD/JPY remained quiet for the second consecutive session on Thursday, hovering around 158.90 in early European trading. The pair appears to be consolidating near the top of a descending channel, with market participants closely watching key technical levels for direction. Momentum is subdued as traders assess broader market sentiment and risk appetite ahead of upcoming economic releases.  

Despite the muted moves, the pair maintains a constructive bullish bias as spot trading stays above both the nine-day and 50-day Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs). This suggests that short-term strength could persist, supporting potential upside near the 159.00 mark, provided the current technical setup holds and no significant external shocks impact the market.
