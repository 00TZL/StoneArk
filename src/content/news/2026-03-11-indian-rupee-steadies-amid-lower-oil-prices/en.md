The Indian Rupee (INR) stabilized on Wednesday as USD/INR remained flat after recovering from earlier losses. The INR found support following a dip in oil prices, which eased pressure on the currency. 

Reports surfaced that the International Energy Agency (IEA) may release record oil reserves to help stabilize global markets. This potential move played a crucial role in boosting investor sentiment, reducing concerns over rising fuel costs, and contributing to the INR's resilience. As a result, the Indian Rupee managed to hold steady, with the USD/INR trading relatively unchanged.
