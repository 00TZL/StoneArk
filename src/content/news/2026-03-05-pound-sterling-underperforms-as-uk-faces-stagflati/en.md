The Pound Sterling is experiencing a downturn, trading lower against its major currency counterparts as concerns about stagflation risks in the UK mount amid ongoing conflict in the Middle East. During Thursday's Asian session, the GBP/USD pair dipped 0.22%, landing at approximately 1.3340.

Market sentiment is weighed down by fears of prolonged economic stagnation coupled with rising inflation, prompting investors to reassess their positions on the UK currency. As geopolitical tensions escalate, the potential impact on global markets continues to add pressure on the Pound, contributing to its underperformance against the USD and other currencies. Traders will closely monitor developments in both the UK economy and international affairs for further cues.
