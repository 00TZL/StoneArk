US Dollar strength extended as growth conditions hold firm, with markets continuing to price in resilient US activity and policy divergence across major central banks.

 analyst Volkmar Baur notes that despite easing oil prices and lower inflation expectations, market pricing still anticipates at least one more Federal Reserve rate hike by year-end, while the European Central Bank is seen delivering only one further move to 2.5%. Policy divergence between the US and euro area continues to underpin dollar resilience in FX markets. Investors remain attentive to incoming data and central bank guidance for confirmation of the rate path ahead. Markets remain focused on inflation trends and policy signaling ahead.
