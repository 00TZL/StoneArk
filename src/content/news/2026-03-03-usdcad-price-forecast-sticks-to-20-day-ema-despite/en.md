**USD/CAD Price Forecast: Holds Steady at 20-Day EMA Amid Middle East Tensions**

The USD/CAD pair is currently hovering around 1.3670 during the late Asian trading session on Tuesday, maintaining a tight trading range. The Canadian Dollar (CAD) is gaining strength, driven by rising oil prices linked to escalating tensions between the United States, Israel, and Iran. This upward momentum in CAD is counterbalancing the overall firmness of the US Dollar (USD).

As the pair consolidates, it continues to respect the 20-day exponential moving average (EMA), indicating a potential resistance level. Market participants are closely monitoring geopolitical developments, which could further influence oil prices and, consequently, the CAD's performance against the USD in the coming days.
