The US Dollar Index (DXY) is trading with a mildly bullish tone, supported by a rising 20-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA), which continues to reinforce positive momentum despite limited gains. The technical outlook remains constructive as the index holds above key support levels, suggesting buyers retain control of the broader trend.

However, the US Dollar (USD) came under modest selling pressure during Tuesday’s European session as renewed optimism surrounding a potential agreement between the United States and Iran improved market sentiment. The prospect of easing geopolitical tensions reduced demand for the safe-haven greenback, although the Dollar’s downside appears limited while the upward-sloping 20-day EMA continues to underpin the bullish bias.
