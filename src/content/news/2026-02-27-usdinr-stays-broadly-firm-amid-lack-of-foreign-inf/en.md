The Indian Rupee (INR) opened flat against the US Dollar (USD) on Friday, staying near its three-week low. The USD/INR pair holds steady around 91.20, reflecting limited movement despite the start of the trading day. 

A lack of foreign inflows into the Indian stock market is weighing on the INR, keeping it under pressure. Investor reluctance to commit capital amid global uncertainties continues to hinder the Indian currency, leaving it vulnerable to a stronger USD in the near term.
