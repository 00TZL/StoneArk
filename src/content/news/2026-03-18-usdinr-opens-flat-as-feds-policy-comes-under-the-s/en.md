The Indian Rupee (INR) is largely unchanged against the US Dollar (USD) as trading opens on Wednesday. The USD/INR pair hovers around the 92.80 mark, reflecting a cautious market sentiment. Investors are holding back, awaiting key developments from the Federal Reserve.

The focus now shifts to the Fed's upcoming monetary policy decision, scheduled for release at 23:30 IST (18:00 GMT). Traders are keenly watching for any signals regarding future interest rate changes, which could have significant implications for currency markets. As a result, market activity is expected to remain subdued until the Fed's announcement provides clearer direction.
