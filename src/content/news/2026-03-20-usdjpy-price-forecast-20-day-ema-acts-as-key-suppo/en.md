The USD/JPY pair is currently trading near 158.33, up 0.4% during the Asian session on Friday. The pair is recovering from Thursday's sharp sell-off, where the Japanese Yen (JPY) struggled despite the Bank of Japan's (BoJ) continued hawkish stance on monetary policy. The BoJ's outlook suggests no immediate shift, but market sentiment remains mixed as the Yen underperforms.

Traders will likely watch this zone closely as it will determine whether the recent recovery can gain traction in the coming sessions.
