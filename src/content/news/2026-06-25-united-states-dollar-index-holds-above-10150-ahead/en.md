United States Dollar Index (DXY) remains firm above the 101.50 mark on Thursday, extending its winning streak for a fourth consecutive session. The index, which tracks the greenback against a basket of six major currencies, is hovering near 101.60 during European trading hours, reflecting sustained demand for the US Dollar ahead of key macroeconomic data releases.

Market participants are closely watching the upcoming US Personal Consumption Expenditures (PCE) inflation report, a key gauge for Federal Reserve policy expectations. The data is expected to influence interest rate outlooks and near-term USD momentum. With risk sentiment still cautious, traders remain positioned for potential volatility in the Dollar Index as inflation signals shape expectations for the central bank’s next policy move this week.
