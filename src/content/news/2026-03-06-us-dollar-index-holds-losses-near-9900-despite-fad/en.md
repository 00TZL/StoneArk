The US Dollar Index (DXY), which tracks the value of the US Dollar (USD) against six major currencies, is trading near 99.00 during the Asian session on Thursday. This marks a continued decline from the previous day's modest gains, as the index struggles to regain upward momentum.

Despite fading expectations for a rate cut by the Federal Reserve, the DXY remains under pressure. Market sentiment is shifting as investors digest mixed economic signals, causing the dollar to hover around the 99.00 mark. The lack of strong bullish catalysts leaves the USD vulnerable to further fluctuations in the short term.
