The British Pound (GBP) is consolidating near a two-month high against the bearish Japanese Yen (JPY) as the market navigates potential intervention risks. After registering impressive gains over the past two days, the GBP/JPY cross is now stabilizing within a defined range, reflecting cautious sentiment among traders.

As the early European session unfolds on Wednesday, market participants are closely monitoring developments that could prompt intervention from Japanese authorities. The JPY's weakness has been a focal point, and any signs of action could lead to increased volatility. Traders will be keen to see if the Pound can maintain its momentum or if external factors will influence the currency pair's trajectory in the coming hours.
