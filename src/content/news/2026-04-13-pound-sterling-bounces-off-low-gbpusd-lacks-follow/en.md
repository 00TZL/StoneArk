The Pound Sterling saw a brief bounce off recent lows as the GBP/USD pair struggled to maintain momentum above the 1.3400 level. After opening with a bearish gap at the start of the week, the pair has ended its five-day winning streak that had driven it to its highest level since late February, around the 1.3485 region set last week.

The recent shift comes amid a firmer US Dollar, which has limited follow-through for GBP/USD beyond the 1.3400 handle. While the pair showed initial resilience, it failed to extend gains, reflecting the market's cautious outlook for the British currency in light of global economic pressures.
