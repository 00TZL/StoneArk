The AUD/USD pair maintained its bullish momentum during the Asian session on Thursday, drawing interest from dip-buyers after the previous day’s pullback from the 0.7275-0.7280 zone—its highest since June 2022. Traders are keeping a close eye on the pair as it hovers near the mid-0.7200s, marking a third consecutive day of gains amid a softer U.S. dollar backdrop.  

Market participants note that the pair’s recent resilience reflects underlying confidence in the Australian dollar, with support seen near 0.7250. While short-term profit-taking may emerge around multi-year highs, the overall bias remains tilted to the upside as AUD/USD continues to test key resistance levels, reinforcing the bullish outlook for the near term.
