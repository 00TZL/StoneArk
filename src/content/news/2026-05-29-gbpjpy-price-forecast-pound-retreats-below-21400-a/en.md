The British Pound (GBP) edged slightly lower against the Japanese Yen (JPY) on Friday, hovering just below the 214.00 level. After dipping to weekly lows around 213.35 on Thursday, GBP/JPY managed a modest rebound but faced resistance, reflecting cautious investor sentiment amid fresh economic data from Japan. Traders are keeping an eye on these levels as the pair consolidates near this round number.  

Japanese economic releases appeared supportive for the yen, lending strength to the currency and putting pressure on the pound. Market participants are weighing the impact of domestic data on Japan’s outlook, which has helped the yen maintain its footing and limited GBP’s upside. Overall, the pair is trading in a tight range, with movements closely linked to incoming macroeconomic signals.
