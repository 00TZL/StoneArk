The AUD/JPY pair is showing a firm footing, trading near 114.65 in early European trade on Thursday. Market participants are positioning cautiously ahead of the high-profile meeting between US President Donald Trump and Chinese President Xi Jinping in Beijing, scheduled for Thursday and Friday. The currency cross has picked up momentum amid broad risk-on sentiment, reflecting optimism that trade discussions could ease tensions between the world’s two largest economies.

Traders are keeping a close eye on developments from the summit, as any positive signals could reinforce the bullish bias for AUD/JPY. Conversely, unresolved trade issues might weigh on the pair. For now, the market appears to favor gains, with technical support levels underpinning the current price action and maintaining a constructive outlook in the near term.
