Swiss Franc edges lower after SNB leaves rates unchanged as expected. The Swiss National Bank held policy steady, meeting forecasts and offering limited surprises to markets. The decision prompted mild risk repricing, with traders adjusting Swiss Franc positions against major peers, particularly the US Dollar, as rate differentials remained unchanged and forward guidance stayed cautious.

The Swiss Franc extended losses against the US Dollar as USD/CHF turned positive on daily charts following the announcement. Earlier intraday gains were fully unwound as market participants recalibrated expectations for near-term monetary divergence. Traders focused on subdued inflation signals and stable policy outlook from Bern, leaving the pair slightly firmer into late trading sessions amid cautious risk sentiment across FX markets overall tone neutral.
