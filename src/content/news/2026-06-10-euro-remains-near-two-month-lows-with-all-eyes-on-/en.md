The Euro (EUR) edged higher against the US Dollar (USD) on Wednesday, marking its third straight day of gains. Despite the modest recovery, the common currency remains under pressure and continues to trade below the former support zone at 1.1575, suggesting that upside momentum remains limited.

Market participants remain focused on the upcoming US Consumer Price Index (CPI) report, which is expected to provide fresh clues on the Federal Reserve’s policy outlook. Ahead of the release, EUR/USD stays close to its lowest levels in two months, with the 1.1500 area remaining within reach as traders assess the balance between Euro resilience and persistent Dollar strength.
