The EUR/GBP pair has pulled back to 0.8635, ending a four-day winning streak as the European session began on Wednesday. The move lower comes as traders adopt a cautious stance ahead of key interest rate decisions from the European Central Bank (ECB) and the Bank of England (BoE) on Thursday.

Market participants are awaiting fresh guidance from both central banks, with the ECB expected to provide insights into its tightening path, while the BoE is anticipated to maintain its hawkish tone. As a result, the EUR/GBP cross remains under pressure, with any shift in central bank policy likely to influence the pair’s next directional move.
