Gold (XAU/USD) shows resilience above the $4,500 level, recovering from a slight dip during the Asian session to the $4,420 region. The precious metal is finding support from a softer U.S. dollar, which continues to bolster its bullish bias. The market remains focused on the potential for further gains following Friday’s impressive rally, which saw gold surge more than 2.50%.

Despite the positive momentum, the bearish potential for gold still seems intact, as market participants remain cautious. The overall outlook suggests that while gold may attempt to build on recent gains, any weakness in the dollar could be offset by broader economic concerns, keeping gold's upward trajectory under scrutiny.
