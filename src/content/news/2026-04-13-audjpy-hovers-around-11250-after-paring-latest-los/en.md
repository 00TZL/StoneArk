The AUD/JPY pair has managed to recover some of its recent losses, currently trading near 112.40 during the Asian session on Monday. After a sharp decline earlier, the Australian dollar has pared its daily losses, though the pair remains in negative territory.

Despite the slight recovery, AUD/JPY continues to face downward pressure as traders adjust to global market conditions. The pair’s movement suggests cautious sentiment, with market participants weighing economic data and geopolitical developments. For now, the 112.50 level serves as a key point of resistance, with further volatility expected in the coming hours.
