The USD/JPY pair remains below the 159.00 level as bulls show caution amid weaker demand for the USD. During Thursday's Asian session, the pair struggled to push past the key resistance near 159.00, failing to sustain the previous day's modest recovery from sub-158.00 levels. This marked a near three-week low for the pair, reflecting the overall hesitation in the market.

The cautious sentiment surrounding the USD is weighing on the pair’s upside momentum. With market participants keeping a close watch on broader economic developments and US data, the USD/JPY pair may continue to face challenges in breaking through the 159.00 resistance unless there is a significant shift in USD strength.
