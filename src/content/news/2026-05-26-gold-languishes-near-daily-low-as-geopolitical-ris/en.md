Gold (XAU/USD) stays under pressure during Tuesday’s European session, hovering near the daily low as rising geopolitical tensions and firm expectations of a hawkish Federal Reserve continue to support the US Dollar. The stronger greenback keeps bullion subdued, with traders remaining cautious ahead of fresh macroeconomic cues and Fed commentary.

The precious metal also struggles to gain traction below the $4,580 horizontal resistance level, which was briefly retested earlier in the day. Persistent USD demand and elevated Treasury yields reduce the appeal of non-yielding assets like gold, leaving XAU/USD vulnerable to further downside in the near term if bullish momentum fails to return.
