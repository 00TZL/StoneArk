Euro extended gains against the US Dollar as expectations around the European Central Bank’s Sintra forum reinforced a broadly hawkish policy outlook. ING strategist Francesco Pesole said policymakers are likely to maintain a firm tone, with markets reassessing the near-term rate path. The Euro benefited from reduced expectations of an imminent policy shift, while traders positioned ahead of upcoming inflation data.

Analysts noted that the Sintra tone combined with sticky inflation risks could keep the Euro supported in the near term especially if June CPI confirms price pressures remain elevated Markets will watch ECB communication closely with any hint of dovish pivot likely to be delayed reinforcing resilience in EUR/USD trading ranges throughout upcoming trading sessions and volatility expected near-term
