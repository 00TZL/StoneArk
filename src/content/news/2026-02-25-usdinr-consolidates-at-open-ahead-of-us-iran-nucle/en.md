The Indian Rupee (INR) opened on a flat note against the US Dollar (USD) this Wednesday, with the pair holding steady in early trade. Market participants are closely watching developments surrounding the ongoing US-Iran nuclear talks, which are expected to influence broader market sentiment in the coming sessions.

While there were no major movements at the start of the day, traders are awaiting further clues from the geopolitical discussions. Any updates from the US-Iran negotiations could impact market volatility and influence the direction of the INR. For now, the currency pair remains in consolidation mode, with cautious trading seen as investors assess the potential outcomes of the diplomatic talks.
