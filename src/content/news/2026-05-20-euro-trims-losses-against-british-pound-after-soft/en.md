EUR/GBP trimmed earlier losses and hovered near 0.8660 during Wednesday’s early European session as the British Pound weakened after softer-than-expected UK inflation data. The Euro found modest support against Sterling, with traders reassessing expectations for future Bank of England policy moves following the inflation release.

The Pound edged lower after the UK consumer price report pointed to easing price pressures, reducing expectations for aggressive monetary tightening. As a result, EUR/GBP recovered part of its intraday decline, while investors continued to monitor incoming economic data and central bank signals for fresh direction in the currency markets.
