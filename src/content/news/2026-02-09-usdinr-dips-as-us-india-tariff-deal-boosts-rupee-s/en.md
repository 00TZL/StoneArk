The USD/INR saw a dip today as the Indian Rupee (INR) gained strength following the announcement of an interim trade deal between the United States and India. The agreement, revealed on Friday, aims to reduce tariffs, reshape energy partnerships, and enhance broader economic cooperation between the two nations. 

This positive development for the Indian economy has provided significant support to the Rupee, boosting investor sentiment and lowering concerns about trade barriers. As a result, the USD/INR exchange rate has softened, reflecting the growing optimism surrounding the trade framework and the potential for further economic collaboration.
