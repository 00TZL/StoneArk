The New Zealand Dollar (NZD) remains under pressure against the US Dollar (USD) on Monday, even as broader market sentiment improves slightly. NZD/USD continues to trade with a mildly bearish bias after failing once again to break above the key 0.5880 resistance zone, which has capped bullish attempts in recent sessions.

The pair’s upside momentum remains limited as investors favor the Greenback amid cautious trading conditions and lingering uncertainty around global growth prospects. While the softer risk tone offers some support to the Kiwi, technical signals suggest sellers are still defending the 0.5880 barrier firmly, keeping the focus on lower support levels in the near term.
