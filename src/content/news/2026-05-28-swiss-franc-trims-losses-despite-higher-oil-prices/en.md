The Swiss Franc (CHF) is holding moderate losses against the US Dollar (USD) on Thursday, though it has recovered most of its earlier decline. The rebound comes despite higher Oil prices, which can weigh on Switzerland’s import-sensitive economy.

Swiss employment data also offered little support, adding to a cautious tone around the currency. Still, the Franc’s ability to trim losses suggests downside pressure remains limited, with traders balancing weaker domestic signals against broader market demand for the safe-haven CHF.
