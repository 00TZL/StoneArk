Investors remained cautious amid uncertainty surrounding the global economic outlook, prompting a rotation away from the Greenback and into traditionally defensive currencies such as the Franc.

As a result, USD/CHF fell to around 0.7760 at the time of writing, down 0.70% on the day. The pair touched its lowest level since September 2011, underscoring the intensity of the current bearish pressure on the US Dollar.
