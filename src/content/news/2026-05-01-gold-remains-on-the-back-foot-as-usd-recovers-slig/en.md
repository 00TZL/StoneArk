Gold (XAU/USD) is struggling to gain traction on Friday, staying on the defensive as the U.S. dollar recovers modestly following yesterday’s sharp dip. Despite the rebound in the greenback, gold prices remain supported above the $4,600 level, showing limited follow-through on earlier losses. Market participants are cautious ahead of key economic data, weighing the dollar’s slight recovery against persistent safe-haven demand for the yellow metal.  

Analysts note that while gold faces short-term pressure, bearish momentum is muted, leaving the market range-bound for now. Investors are watching U.S. Treasury yields and geopolitical developments closely, factors that could influence XAU/USD direction. With limited conviction, gold may continue oscillating around current levels until more decisive catalysts emerge.
