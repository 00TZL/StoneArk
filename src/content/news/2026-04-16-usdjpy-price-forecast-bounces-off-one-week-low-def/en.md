USD/JPY faced renewed selling pressure after a brief uptick on Wednesday, retreating to a one-week low near 158.25 during Thursday's Asian session. The pair saw fresh bearish momentum as investors remained cautious following the previous day's modest rise, which failed to break significant resistance.

Despite the pullback, USD/JPY managed to defend key support around the 158.25 level, keeping its trading range intact for the time being. The outlook remains uncertain as market participants weigh global risk sentiment and key economic data, with the next major support level seen at 158.00. Traders will be watching closely for further developments in the coming sessions.
