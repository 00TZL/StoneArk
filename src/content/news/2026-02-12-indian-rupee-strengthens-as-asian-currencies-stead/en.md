The Indian Rupee (INR) saw a slight strengthening on Thursday, as the USD/INR pair weakened after modest gains in the previous session. The Rupee found support, which helped the currency stabilize despite global market movements. 

Asian currencies remained largely steady, even with rising US Treasury yields following stronger-than-expected jobs data from the United States. While the data indicated a robust job market, the broader impact on the INR was limited, with the currency managing to hold its ground against the dollar. The steady performance of Asian currencies further contributed to the INR's resilience.
