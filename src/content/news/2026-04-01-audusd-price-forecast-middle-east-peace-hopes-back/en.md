The AUD/USD pair retraced some of its earlier gains but remains 0.12% higher, trading close to 0.6910 in the late Asian session on Wednesday. The pair had initially gained momentum, bolstered by positive market sentiment surrounding Middle East peace developments, which provided support for risk-sensitive assets like the Aussie dollar.

Despite the pullback, hopes for peace in the Middle East continue to offer support, driving the pair towards further recovery. The focus now shifts to whether the AUD/USD can maintain this momentum and break through the 20-day EMA, a key resistance level. Traders will likely keep an eye on broader risk sentiment and any fresh developments that could influence price action in the coming sessions.
