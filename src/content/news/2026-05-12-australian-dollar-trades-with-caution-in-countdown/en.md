The Australian Dollar (AUD) is trading cautiously in early Asian sessions on Tuesday, edging down 0.23% to around 0.7230 against the US Dollar (USD). Investors appear hesitant as the market braces for potential volatility, with attention sharply focused on the upcoming Australian Budget for 2026. The currency’s modest retreat reflects a wait-and-see sentiment, as traders weigh economic forecasts and fiscal plans that could influence monetary policy expectations.

Market participants are positioning themselves ahead of the 09:30 GMT budget release, which could set the tone for short-term AUD movements. Analysts suggest that any surprises in spending plans or revenue projections may trigger swift reactions in the forex market. For now, the Australian Dollar remains under cautious pressure, with traders closely monitoring developments that could reshape risk sentiment in the antipodean currency.
