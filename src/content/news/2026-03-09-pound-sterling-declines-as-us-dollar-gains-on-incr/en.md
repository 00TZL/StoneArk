The Pound Sterling (GBP) has slipped against the US Dollar (USD), reversing some of the modest gains seen in the previous session. GBP/USD is currently trading around 1.3300 during the Asian trading hours on Monday, marking a decline as risk sentiment shifts in global markets.

The US Dollar strengthens as investors flock to safe-haven assets, driven by rising uncertainty surrounding the ongoing conflict in Iran. With no clear resolution in sight, concerns over geopolitical instability continue to weigh on risk appetite, pushing the greenback higher while the British currency struggles to maintain its footing.
